Based in Riverside Industrial Park

Run out of the yard, not a call centre.

Total Motor Maintenance operates out of Riverside Industrial Park in Nelspruit, where cars and trucks are worked on side by side. Walter Ngomane runs the emergency line personally, so when you call after hours, you're talking to the person coming out to you.

Two landlines cover the workshop for bookings, and the mobile line stays open for breakdowns, any time of day or night.
